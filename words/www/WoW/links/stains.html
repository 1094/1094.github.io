stains heard

there is this echo
an echo
an echo of
repeating before my mind
thoughts echo, the same word
the same words, the same
thought, walking, then skipping
skipping words, skipping thoughts
running thru my mind of echos
images don't fade, they stain
echos of colors - blood red
then rose, lily pink, some type of orange
an echo, repeating, an acho
no one heard the first time,
the first time it sounded
like an echo, & again
thoughts, walking, skipping
running circle thru endless color
these thoughts, they echo, canyon like
mind of holes, thoughts roll into, out of
echos of lives, not mine, stories, dreams
the fight never ends, it just echos
of weeks past, & again
that pause,
wait for it

. . .

an echo
