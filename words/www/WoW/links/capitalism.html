capitalism

Fearful, she held her hands over her naked breast
aware of the sounds around her, but lost to their meaning.
Thousands of people with pale skin stood below her stage,
taking turns raising their hands, nodding their heads, and smiling,
strange twisted smiles, with secret stuff behind them.
Cold, confused, where were her children, her husband?
A few men walked on the stage beside her, circling like vultures.
What did they want? Their lips moved as they grinned,
as they took her arms, legs, and breasts into their hands,
pulling flexing, squeezing --

The wench yelled something
tainted and loud, or they thought it was foul, so they
began the training, quick and without mercy.
Three days later she awoke in a room not so much
unlike the one on the boat, to a language she understood.
It's been weeks that felt like years, her ears drank of that water
"Be still. Later I'll teach you the rules." So she lay still
as stones in cold water.

-- Her eyes grew big, and she tried
to pull away, one are raised, quick to smack her,
"No hitting the merchandise until you've paid."
What are they saying? What language is this?
BANG, a man with a hammer slams it down
and a big pale man came and took her away by the chain
hanging off her neck, linking her arms with her legs.
