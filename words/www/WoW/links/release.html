damn, I need a girl
cuz this lust ain't going nowhere
at least if I had a focus
a center to my attention
I could release,
relax, rebuild,
and release again
release til my fingers cramp
til my jaw refuses to move
til my tongue is too tired to talk
relax, then rebuild, then again

the release
