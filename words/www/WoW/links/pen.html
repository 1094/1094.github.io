pen & paper

this pen is the only voice i have
sounds and handshapes continue to fail me
even the most thought out ideas
crumble under the weight of speaking
controlling my daydreams is too much work
even when i dream in ASL the feeling is gone
& this paper only conveys so much
but this is all i have
this pen & paper
it's all that counts, all that matters
clothes will fade, machines will break
the song will end & the page will turn
but this pen & paper
will forever create new
will forever be under my control
will forever move smoothly against one another

this paper is is the only voice i have
and this pen allows it to be heard
